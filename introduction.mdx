Introducing LangBear, a groundbreaking open-source AI prompt management platform. Designed to
enhance the agility and efficacy of AI applications, LangBear allows for **on-the-fly editing
and deployment of prompts to production**. Beyond simple management, it offers an array of
features to elevate prompt quality, including **monitoring**, **scoring**, **online evaluation**,
and **A/B testing** capabilities.

With LangBear, users gain the tools to refine and optimize AI prompts
swiftly and efficiently, empowering continuous improvement in real-world applications.
